SHAKTI
engine

Advanced Propulsion Technology

Shakti is a modern bi-propellant rocket engine designed for high reliability and affordable production. It uses SLM-based additive manufacturing to cut part count, reduce mass, and avoid complex fabrication steps. Its electric-pump architecture removes the need for turbopumps and gives clean, predictable control during operation.

Nine Shakti engines work together on the first stage to deliver strong lift capability with natural redundancy and smooth throttle response. For upper-stage missions, a vacuum-optimized Shakti variant provides higher efficiency and better burn stability. The system supports rapid orbital deployment and meets fast-response defense requirements with dependable and repeatable performance.
